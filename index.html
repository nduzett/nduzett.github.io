I am a 5th-year economics PhD candidate at Texas A&M University. I received a BS in Economics and Mathematics from Brigham Young University in 2020, and worked as a pre-doc researcher in BYU's Record Linking Lab from 2020-2021.

My research focuses on public economics and political economy, using quasi-experimental and machine-learning methods in the local government setting to learn about group behavior and decision-making.
